---
question: "What is a Fractional CMO?"
order: 2
---

A Fractional CMO is a part-time senior leader responsible for marketing strategy, sales alignment, and team direction. You get executive-level guidance and accountability at a lower cost than a permanent hire. It works well for startups and scaleups that need experienced leadership across marketing, sales, and ecommerce but aren't ready to commit to a full-time salary.
