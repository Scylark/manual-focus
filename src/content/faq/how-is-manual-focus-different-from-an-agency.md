---
question: "How is Manual Focus different from a traditional agency?"
order: 3
---

A traditional agency hires a team to execute campaigns for you and bills you for their time. Manual Focus is a small senior team that uses AI as leverage to deliver function-team output. There's no account manager layer, no junior staff working your account, and no agency overhead. You're working directly with the operators, and the work ships faster because AI does the parts a junior team would.
